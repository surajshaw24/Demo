We Bring Out Your Natural Beauty

Founded in 2015, LUXE Beauty Studio has become a premier destination for makeup artistry and beauty treatments in Jharkhand. Our team of certified professionals is dedicated to helping you look and feel your absolute best for every special occasion.

We use only high-quality, cruelty-free products from top brands like MAC, Huda Beauty, and Charlotte Tilbury that are carefully selected to suit all skin types and tones. Our studio is designed to provide a luxurious, relaxing experience from the moment you walk in through our doors.
