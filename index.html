About Sunidhi Makeover & Academy

With over 8 years of experience in the beauty industry, I've dedicated my career to helping clients discover their most beautiful selves through expert makeup application, skincare, and beauty education.

What began as a passion for artistry has grown into a thriving academy where I train the next generation of beauty professionals in advanced techniques while continuing to provide luxury makeup services for brides and special events.

My approach combines technical precision with an artistic eye to enhance your natural features while creating the look you envision.
